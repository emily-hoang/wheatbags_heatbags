Relaxation of Muscles and Tendons, Relieve Pain Naturally About Our Wheat Bags, Heat Bags

Wheat bags have been used to assist in the management of pain in a natural, effective, comforting and affordable manner for many years.

Wheat has a unique cellular structure which can absorb heat or cold quickly and then release it evenly. This makes a wheat bag a wonderful and safe alternative to a hot water bottle.

Simply heat in a microwave on full heat for 2 minutes (times vary) or alternatively cool in the fridge.
